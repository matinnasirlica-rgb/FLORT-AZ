Our Story

Cafe Meets
Japanese Craft

Cafe WA Sushi is a cozy neighbourhood spot in North York where cafe culture meets Japanese cuisine. Whether you're grabbing a morning bagel with coffee or picking up a fresh sushi bento for lunch, we bring both worlds together in one warm, welcoming space.

"WA" (和) means harmony in Japanese — and that's exactly what we aim for: a harmonious blend of comfort food and sushi artistry, served fast and fresh.
